About me

Hi there!
My name is Andrew and I am a Full Stack Software Engineer. I started my journey studying Biology with a focus on neuroscience at Stony Brook University (Go Seawolves!!). After obtaining my Bachelor of Science, I worked in both the healthcare and biotech industry where I was exposed to the strengths of software and their powerful applications in the real world. I discovered that programming can be a powerful tool used to improve the lives of those in need, whether in healthcare, reagent production laboratories, or any other setting. This motivated me to learn how to build software myself and led me to transition into software engineering with growing ambition to learn more.

I enjoy learning new skills and technologies, exploring innovative solutions and building user-intuitive applications that help solve real world problems. When I'm not coding, you'll find me in the kitchen discovering new recipes to cook.
